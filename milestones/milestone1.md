## Reflection

### 1. What went well during this unit?
What went well during this unit was our group dynamic and idea selection process. After brainstorming several project ideas, we all naturally gravitated toward the same one and agreed on what would work best for the scope of this project. It was also helpful that everyone was open-minded and collaborative rather than trying to push one idea over the others.

### 2. What were some challenges your group faced in this unit?
One challenge we faced was figuring out how to divide responsibilities, since we all wanted to contribute to every part of the project. At first, it was difficult to assign roles in a way that felt balanced and effective. Once we started breaking the work into more specific tasks, we were able to divide our efforts more clearly, such as splitting up the graphics prototype and the entity relationship diagram.

### 3. What additional support will you need in upcoming units as you continue to work on your final project?
As we continue working on our final project, additional support in the form of feedback on our ideas, design decisions, and current progress would be very helpful. Having guidance as we move from planning into implementation will help us stay on track and make strong technical choices.
