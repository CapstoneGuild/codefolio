## Reflection

### 1. What went well during this unit?

Our communication improved significantly this week. We met twice as a group, stayed active on Slack, and made strong progress toward completing the main features of our project. We covered a substantial amount of development work, and the project is now approaching feature‑complete status.

### 2. What were some challenges your group faced in this unit?

We encountered several challenges while deploying our backend on Render and our frontend on Netlify for testing. Some pull requests were delayed while waiting for team member approvals. Additionally, one group member became unresponsive for nearly two weeks, which required the rest of the team to redistribute and complete their remaining tasks to keep the project on track.

### Which features and user stories would you consider “at risk”? How will you change your plan if those items remain “at risk”?

These include deleting and editing comments, post likes and bookmarking, direct messaging between users, and advanced filtering for search results. If these remain at risk, we will focus on delivering a stable core experience and move optional social features to stretch goals.
